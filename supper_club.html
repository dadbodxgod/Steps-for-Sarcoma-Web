From Friends to Family

Sarcoma Supper Club is not only a way to support an amazing cause, but also to enjoy time with friends!

How it Works

Every month friends volunteer to host. Guests will pay $5 to the chef for ingredients and $10 to the Steps for Sarcoma fund.
